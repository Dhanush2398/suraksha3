About Suraksha Home Care

Established in 2019, Suraksha Home Care embarked on a mission to elevate home cleanliness by offering top-notch household cleaning products and concentrated cleaners. Over the years, our commitment to quality has propelled us to become a trusted cleaning chemicals manufacturer, serving a growing clientele across India.

At Suraksha Home Care, we are committed to providing safe, effective, and eco-friendly home care solutions. Our range of products is designed to keep your living spaces fresh, hygienic, and chemical-free — ensuring a healthier environment for your loved ones.

Built on trust, quality, and customer satisfaction, we continue to innovate and bring sustainable cleaning products that make a difference — not just in your home, but for the planet too.

Why Choose Suraksha Home Care?

We are committed to delivering safe, effective, and eco-friendly home care solutions you can trust.
